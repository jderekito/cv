I am an urban geographer/GIScientist with a research interest in the behavioral and legal geography of public space (e.g., beach access, representation in electoral geography, and crime mapping). 
To study these topics, I employ spatial ethnographic (e.g. sketch mapping and map usability studies) and exploratory spatial anlaysis (e.g., time geography and Bayes nets/GIS integration)
