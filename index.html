Research:

I am a quantitative human geographer and Geographic Information Scientist. The core of my research is on the study of the ways in which cartography and exploratory spatial data analysis (ESDA) can be used to explore the human experience of place, with applications to topics within the sub-disiplines of urban and political geography. Specifically, my research addresses the following:
